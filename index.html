BloodBridge ("we", "our", "us") operates the BloodBridge mobile application and website (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service — and the choices you have associated with that data. By using BloodBridge, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1

Information We Collect

We collect different types of information to provide and improve our Service to you. The table below outlines what we collect and why.

Data Type Examples Status
Identity Full name, profile photo, date of birth Required
Contact Phone number, email address, city Required
Health Blood group, last donation date, medical conditions Required
Location City, approximate GPS (for nearby matching) Optional
Usage Data Pages visited, features used, session duration Automatic
Device Info Device model, OS version, push token Automatic
Communications Messages sent via in-app chat or SOS alerts Optional

We never collect financial or payment information. BloodBridge is a humanitarian platform — no transactions of money occur on the app.

2

How We Use Your Data

BloodBridge uses collected data for the following purposes:

Core Uses

• Donor Matching: Connect blood seekers with compatible, available donors in their area based on blood type and location.

• Blood Bank Discovery: Show nearby blood banks and live stock levels to users in need.

• Emergency Alerts: Send push notifications for urgent blood requests matching your blood group.

• Account Management: Create and manage your donor/requester profile securely.

• Analytics & Improvement: Understand how users interact with the app to improve features and performance.

We process your data based on your consent, the performance of a contract (our Terms of Service), and legitimate interests in operating a life-saving platform.

3

Your phone number is only visible to another user when you explicitly choose to respond to a blood request or initiate contact. It is never listed publicly.

4

Health & Sensitive Data

Blood group and health-related information is classified as sensitive personal data. We handle it with the highest standards:

Our Commitments

• Health data is stored with AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit.

• Health information is never used for advertising, profiling, or sold to any third party.

• Access to health data within our systems is strictly role-based and logged.

• You may request deletion of your health data at any time from your profile settings.

By registering as a donor or posting a blood request, you explicitly consent to BloodBridge processing your blood group information for the sole purpose of life-saving coordination.

5

Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include:

Security Measures

🔐  Encryption: AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit

🔑  Authentication: OTP-based login, no passwords stored

🛡️  Access Control: Role-based internal access with audit logs

🔍  Monitoring: 24/7 intrusion detection & anomaly alerts

📋  Audits: Annual third-party security audits

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8

Children's Privacy

BloodBridge is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. Blood donation requires medical eligibility, and our Service is not directed at minors.

If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 18, we will delete that information immediately. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us at privacy@bloodbridge.app.
